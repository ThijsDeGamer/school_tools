METHODS OF PLAY

This game can be played from your internet browser, a stand-alone Flash Player, or by the included EXE (see instructions below).

You must have Flash Player installed to play the game from your internet browser.

Flash Player version requirements are Flash Player 8 or higher. This game has been tested to work in Flash Player 8 through 11.




-- HOW TO PLAY --

HOW TO OPEN IN AN INTERNET BROWSER:

Open "Launcher" HTML Document (Launcher.html) in your internet browser.




HOW TO OPEN IN A STAND-ALONE FLASH PLAYER:

Open "Launcher" Shockwave Flash Object (Launcher.swf) in your stand-alone Flash Player.




HOW TO OPEN EXE:

Open "Launcher" Adobe Flash Player application (Launcher.exe).
