ON PATROL OFFLINE EDITION - READ ME


IMPORTANT NOTICE

Do not rename, rearrange, move, or delete any of the files or folders for this game. Doing so will cripple the game.

This game saves by a Local Shared Object. If you move the game to a different folder location, it may loose reference to you existing SOL file.




METHODS OF PLAY

This game can be played from your internet browser, a stand-alone Flash Player, or by the included EXE's (see instructions below).

You must have Flash Player installed to play the game from your internet browser.

Version requirements are Flash Player 10 or higher. This game has been tested to work in Flash Player 10.




-- HOW TO PLAY --


HOW TO OPEN IN AN INTERNET BROWSER:

Open "Launcher" HTML Document (Launcher.html) in your internet browser.




HOW TO OPEN EXE:

Open "Launcher" Application (Launcher.exe).




HOW TO OPEN IN A STAND-ALONE FLASH PLAYER:

Open "Launcher" Shockwave Flash Object (Launcher.swf).
